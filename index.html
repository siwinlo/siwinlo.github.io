Siwin Lo

Bio
Siwin Lo is a New York-based, Vancouver-raised writer and PhD candidate in Art History at the City University of New York's Graduate Center. Her writing has appeared in The Brooklyn Rail, e-flux, and Canadian Art Magazine. She holds a BA (2013) and MA (2015) from the University of British Columbia. Her MA thesis problematizes artistic labour in the abstract paintings of Agnes Martin.
