About Me

I'm an art historian turned aspiring software engineer. I hold an MA and BA in Art History from the University of British Columbia, in Vancouver, Canada. I also write, and my work has been published in The Brooklyn Rail, e-flux, and Canadian Art. Recently, I have been learning some basic coding skills because I want to build digital tools that people can use. In my spare time, I like to go rockclimbing.

Projects

What Should I See This Week is a React app that picks a random show for you to see this week from an external API.
